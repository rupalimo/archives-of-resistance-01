is an online project curated by soJin Chun to bring together contemporary artists that work with alternative archives as raw material for their work.

This online project is coordinated in Tkaronto (Toronto) as a means to connect artists from across the Americas. Artists present works that illustrate stories of resistance providing an intimate and nuanced look at the community history they represent. Each artist, team of artists and collective will present their own solo online exhibition showcased between October 2022 to March 2023. In tandem, we will host online artist talks, panel discussions and workshops.

Other collaborators of this project are the Canadian Filmmakers Distribution Centre, and White Water Gallery

This project is funded by the Canada Council for the Arts and the Ontario Arts Council.
